Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What ingredients do you use in your drinks?

We use fresh, high-quality ingredients sourced from local farms. Our drinks are made with fruits, vegetables, and natural sweeteners.

2. Are your drinks suitable for people with dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer a variety of drinks that cater to different dietary needs, including vegan, gluten-free, and low-sugar options. Please check the labels for specific information.

3. Can I customize my drink?

Absolutely! You can customize your drink by choosing your preferred ingredients and add-ons. Just let our staff know your preferences when ordering.

4. Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services through our website and partner apps. Check our delivery options for more details.

5. How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can reach out to us through our contact form or email us directly at support@nutridrink.com.
